Eye Piece is a PC game made in Unreal Engine 5.5.4.
It lets you explore an incredible story across multiple moments in time and space.

Explore 5 intricately designed environments, each reflecting a different moment and atmosphere, from a late 1977 family home.

In this game, you play as a detective who can travel through time to solve a mystery. The gameplay focuses on exploration and puzzle-solving, with a strong emphasis on narrative and atmosphere.
